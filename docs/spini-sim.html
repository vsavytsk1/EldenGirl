the reading material comes first

Most of this violence never reaches a report. Under 40% of women who experience it seek any help; under 10% of those go to the police. The official numbers are a sliver of the real ones.

The abuse that never gets counted is exactly the abuse a person has to recognise for herself — unnamed, it routes back to “something is wrong with me.” That is what the training ground is for: to make the moveset nameable, silently, in her own head.

An app cannot solve this. It can make the patterns nameable and a number reachable. Leaving safely takes people — advocates, friends, shelters, lawyers. Every screen points outward.
